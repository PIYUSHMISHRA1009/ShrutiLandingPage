Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about the 45-day program for law students and legal professionals.

Who is this program designed for?

This 45-day program is specifically designed for law students and legal professionals who want to build their personal brand, enhance their professional presence, and prepare for career opportunities in the legal field.

When does the October batch start?

The October batch starts on 5th October and runs every Sunday for 6 weeks. Each session is conducted via Google Meet with recordings provided for those who cannot attend live.

What is included in the ₹7,000 program fee?

The fee includes 6 weekly group sessions, personalized one-on-one sessions (first 2 weeks), one-on-one mock interviews (final week), WhatsApp group access, session recordings, and all resources including CV templates, LinkedIn guides, and research materials.

How much time do I need to commit each week?

Each Sunday session runs for approximately 2 hours. Additionally, you'll receive weekly personalized tasks that may require 1-2 hours of your time for completion and practice.

Will I get individual attention in this group program?

Yes! The program includes personalized one-on-one sessions during the first 2 weeks and individual mock interview sessions in the final week, ensuring you receive tailored guidance alongside the group learning.

Can beginners with no experience join this program?

Absolutely! This program is designed for all levels - from law students just starting their career journey to legal professionals looking to enhance their skills and professional presence.

What happens after the 45-day program ends?

You'll receive 3 months of continuous support to help you achieve your first ₹10,000 milestone, optimize your strongest skills, and prepare confidently for opportunities.

Are the sessions recorded if I miss a live session?

Yes, all Google Meet sessions are recorded and shared with participants. However, we highly recommend attending live sessions for maximum interaction and learning experience.

How will this program help me earn my first ₹10,000?

The program covers freelancing strategies, CV optimization, cold outreach techniques, and skill development. However, earnings are not guaranteed and depend on your effort and implementation of the strategies taught.

Is there any support available between sessions?

Yes! You'll have access to a dedicated WhatsApp group for quick questions, updates, and peer support throughout the program duration.
